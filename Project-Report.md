## Task 4 - Email Etiquette

A situation where poor digital communication can cause a real problem is when a student sends an email to a professor without a clear subject line or proper greeting. For example, if a student writes a vague message like “Send details” without context, the professor may not understand the request or may ignore the email due to its unprofessional tone.

This can lead to delays, missed information, or even loss of opportunities. In academic and professional environments, clear and respectful communication is very important.

This situation could have been avoided by following proper email etiquette, such as writing a clear subject line, addressing the recipient respectfully, and explaining the purpose of the email in a structured manner. Using polite language and proper formatting ensures that the message is understood correctly and creates a positive impression.c
Overall, this task helped me gain practical experience with useful digital tools that support learning and collaboration.

## Task 5 - Cybercrime

While researching cybercrime, I was most surprised by how easily people can become victims of online fraud, especially UPI scams. Many attacks do not require advanced technical skills and rely on simple tricks like fake links, calls, or payment requests to deceive users. It showed me that lack of awareness is one of the biggest reasons behind such incidents.

This made me realize the importance of being cautious while using digital platforms, especially for financial transactions. One habit I will personally change is being more careful before clicking on any links or approving payment requests. I will make sure to verify the source and never share sensitive information such as OTPs or passwords.

Overall, this task increased my awareness and encouraged me to adopt safer online practices in my daily life.

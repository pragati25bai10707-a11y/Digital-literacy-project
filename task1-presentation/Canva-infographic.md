# Description

For this project,I used Canva to design my Digital Literacy Awareness poster. Canva made it easy to choose a clean layout, add icons, and organize information in a visually appealing way. My design focuses on spreading awareness about essential digital skills and safe online practices. It includes a brief explanation of digital literacy, commonly used digital tools like Docs, Drive, Canva, and Zoom, along with important safety tips such as using strong passwords, enabling two-factor authentication, and being cautious of phishing emails. I also added a section on email etiquette to highlight responsible communication online.
One thing I found interesting while making this design was how visual elements like icons and colors can make information more engaging and easier to understand. It helped me realize the importance of presenting information clearly, not just writing it. However, I found it slightly difficult to maintain a balance between adding enough information and keeping the design neat and uncluttered. Overall, this project improved both my digital awareness and my design skills.

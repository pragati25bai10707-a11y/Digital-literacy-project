# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone  
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links  
- Always verify payment requests before approving  
- Use strong passwords for all accounts  
- Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible  
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions  
- Regularly check your bank statements for unusual activity  
- Install apps only from trusted sources  
- Do not respond to calls claiming to be from bank officials asking for details  
- Report fraud immediately on https://cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930  

## UPI Safety Tips
- Never approve a "collect request" unless you are sure  
- Remember: receiving money does NOT require entering your UPI PIN
